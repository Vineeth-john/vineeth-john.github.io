IoT based Liver disease detection using
Convolutional Neural Networks

Liver diseases are a significant health concern and a leading cause of mortality worldwide. Among the various liver diseases, liver tumors are particularly noteworthy. These tumors represent abnormal and uncontrolled growths of liver cells, which can develop on the surface or within the liver itself. Liver tumors can be benign or malignant, with the latter posing a serious threat due to its potential to spread to other parts of the body. Early detection and effective treatment are crucial in managing liver tumors and improving patient outcomes.

By actively identifying and mitigating these biases, we can create more inclusive and representative models. Furthermore, promoting transparency and accountability in the creation and deployment of these models is vital for fostering trust and integrity in AI systems.

    The stock market is a dynamic and complex system, influenced by countless factors ranging from economic indicators to investor sentiment. Predicting stock prices is a challenging task that requires advanced analytical techniques due to the market's inherent volatility and unpredictability. In this project, I utilize Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks to forecast Tesla's stock price. LSTM, a type of recurrent neural network, excels in processing and predicting time series data. By analyzing historical stock prices and financial indicators, this project aims to provide accurate predictions, helping investors and analysts navigate the uncertainties of the stock market.
